06 — Endowments & Foundations Channel
Advisors to Nonprofits & Institutional Sponsors
A small but growing segment of financial advisors work primarily in — or are expanding into — the endowments and foundations channel. These advisors don't just serve individual donors: they advise the nonprofit organizations themselves, helping community foundations, federations, and mission-driven institutions make decisions about investment strategy, platform infrastructure, and program design. For these advisors, GiftingNetwork opens a distinct institutional opportunity. By introducing a nonprofit client to the GiftingNetwork platform, the referring advisor can negotiate to have their firm's investment-managed products set as the default investment options across the sponsor's entire fund lineup — from day one of onboarding. Every new donor fund launched at that sponsor defaults to the advisor's managed products. The advisory fee relationship operates at the sponsor level, not the individual donor level, and grows as the sponsor's program grows.

● New Product Line
GiftingPension

Long-term income for
younger clients,
powered by giving

GiftingPension gives wealth management firms a compelling new tool: Pooled Income Funds (PIFs) structured specifically for younger, high-net-worth clients. Clients contribute assets today, receive income for life, and ultimately direct the remainder to charity — creating a lifelong philanthropic and financial planning touchpoint for the advisor relationship.

✓
Income for life from day one. Younger clients contribute assets to the PIF and immediately begin receiving pro-rata income distributions based on the fund's investment return — a genuine long-term income stream, not a hypothetical future benefit.
✓
The advisor manages the investment pool. Unlike most charitable giving vehicles, the financial advisor retains management of the pooled assets — building a long-term investment relationship that spans the full duration of the PIF, often decades. It's among the stickiest AUM an advisor can hold.
✓
Deepens advisor relationships early. PIFs create a decades-long engagement between advisor, client, and the charitable mission — locking in relationship continuity across generational wealth events.
✓
GiftingNetwork handles the infrastructure. Fund administration, investment pooling, IRS compliance, income distributions, and reporting — all managed through our platform so wealth management firms offer PIFs without operational overhead.

About GiftingNetwork

Built by people who believe
philanthropy deserves better tools

GiftingNetwork was founded on a simple conviction: the infrastructure powering charitable giving in America was too fragmented, too expensive, and too inaccessible to the organizations doing the most important work. We set out to change that.

Headquartered in Morristown, New Jersey, with operations in the US and India, GiftingNetwork is an independent, founder-led company built to serve institutional sponsors, financial advisors, and the donors they work with — without the conflicts that come from being owned by the commercial players we compete against.

🧭
Sponsor-First
We serve the institutional sponsor — not the national commercial provider — and build our product accordingly.
🔓
Independent
Founder-led, unconflicted, and accountable only to the sponsors, advisors, and donors we serve.
⚙️
Infrastructure
We're not a donor-facing app. We're the operating system underneath the philanthropic ecosystem.
🌎
Scale
Built to handle the complexity of large community foundations while remaining accessible to emerging sponsors.
