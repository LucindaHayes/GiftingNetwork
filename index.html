GiftingPension

Long-term income for
younger clients,
powered by giving

GiftingPension gives wealth management firms a compelling new tool: Pooled Income Funds (PIFs) structured specifically for younger, high-net-worth clients. Clients contribute assets today, receive income for life, and ultimately direct the remainder to charity, creating a lifelong philanthropic and financial planning touchpoint for the advisor relationship.

✓
Income for life from day one. Younger clients contribute assets to the PIF and immediately begin receiving pro-rata income distributions based on the fund's investment return, a genuine long-term income stream, not a hypothetical future benefit.
✓
The advisor manages the investment pool. Unlike most charitable giving vehicles, the financial advisor retains management of the pooled assets, building a long-term investment relationship that spans the full duration of the PIF, often decades. It's among the stickiest AUM an advisor can hold.
✓
Deepens advisor relationships early. PIFs create a decades-long engagement between advisor, client, and the charitable mission, locking in relationship continuity across generational wealth events.
✓
GiftingNetwork handles the infrastructure. Fund administration, investment pooling, IRS compliance, income distributions, and reporting, all managed through our platform so wealth management firms offer PIFs without operational overhead.
How It Works
1
Younger client contributes assets to the PIF
Cash, securities, or appreciated assets, receives immediate partial tax deduction
2
Assets are pooled & professionally invested
GiftingNetwork manages fund administration, IRS compliance & reporting
3
Client receives income distributions for life
Pro-rata share of fund earnings, a genuine long-term income stream
4
Remainder passes to charity at end of life
Client designates the charitable beneficiary, mission fulfilled
Offer PIFs to your younger clients, without the overhead.
GiftingPension is designed for wealth management firms and financial advisors who want to bring Pooled Income Funds to a new generation of philanthropic clients. GiftingNetwork handles all administration, you deepen the relationship.
